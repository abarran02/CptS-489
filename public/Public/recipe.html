Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

1. 2 bread slices

2. Cheese

3. Butter

Instructions:

Butter the one side of each bread slice. Place a slice of bread butter side down onto medium hot pan. Place cheese on top of bread. Place the last bread slice butter side up on cheese. Flip when bread is seared.

Prep Time: 3 mins

Cook Time: 4 mins
